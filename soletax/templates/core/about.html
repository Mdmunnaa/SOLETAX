About SoleTax

Built for UK freelancers, by people who understand the struggle.

Our Story

SoleTax was born out of frustration. UK sole traders — plumbers, designers, photographers, writers — were spending hundreds of pounds every year on accountants just to file basic tax returns. Or worse, they were drowning in spreadsheets, losing receipts, and missing deadlines.

We built SoleTax to fix that. A clean, simple tool that does the three things UK freelancers actually need: invoicing, expense tracking, and HMRC-compatible tax reports. Nothing more, nothing less.

Our Mission

To make tax compliance so simple that every UK sole trader can do it themselves — saving time, saving money, and staying stress-free.
