Our Story

SoleTax was born out of frustration. UK sole traders — plumbers, designers, photographers, writers — were spending hundreds of pounds every year on accountants just to file basic tax returns. Or worse, drowning in spreadsheets and missing deadlines.

We built SoleTax to fix that. A clean, simple tool that does the three things UK freelancers actually need: invoicing, expense tracking, and HMRC-compatible tax reports.

Our Mission

To make tax compliance so simple that every UK sole trader can do it themselves — saving time, saving money, and staying completely stress-free.
