FAQ

Common questions.

What is Making Tax Digital (MTD)?
MTD is HMRC's digital record-keeping initiative. From April 2026, UK sole traders earning over £50k/year must submit tax updates quarterly through MTD-compatible software. SoleTax generates those reports automatically.
Do I still need an accountant?
For most sole traders, no. SoleTax handles invoicing, expense tracking, and generates HMRC-compatible tax reports. For complex cases (VAT, multiple income streams), a brief accountant consultation may still help.
Is my financial data safe?
Completely. Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, stored in UK-based servers, and never sold or shared with third parties. You can export or permanently delete your data at any time under GDPR.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — cancel any time from your account settings. No cancellation fees, no awkward phone calls. We also have a 30-day money-back guarantee, full stop.
Does it work for VAT-registered businesses?
SoleTax currently targets non-VAT sole traders. VAT support is in development — sign up to be notified when it launches.
