FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is Making Tax Digital (MTD)?
Making Tax Digital is HMRC's initiative requiring UK sole traders to keep digital records and submit tax updates quarterly. From April 2026, it becomes mandatory for sole traders earning over £50,000/year.
Do I need an accountant if I use SoleTax?
Most sole traders won't need one for basic tax filing. SoleTax generates HMRC-compatible reports automatically. However, for complex situations (multiple income streams, VAT registration), we recommend consulting a professional.
Is my financial data safe?
Absolutely. Your data is encrypted, stored securely, and never shared or sold to third parties. You can export or delete your data at any time.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime with no penalty. We also offer a 30-day money back guarantee — if you're not satisfied, we'll refund you, no questions asked.
Does SoleTax work for VAT-registered businesses?
SoleTax is currently designed for non-VAT-registered UK sole traders. VAT support is on our roadmap for later this year.
